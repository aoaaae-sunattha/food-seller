Tech Stack
Next.js (App Router) Vercel (hosting) Google Sheets API Claude Vision API (OCR) Google OAuth LINE Bot (backup)
Screens (6)
1.Dashboard — income this week, expenses, low stock alerts, quick nav
2.Upload Receipt — photo → Claude Vision extracts French items → confirm → saves to Sheets
3.Stock Deduction — pick menus (+ Other) → ingredients pre-filled per menu section → adjustable → reason field
4.Daily Sales — boxes per dish + cash/card totals → end-of-day settlement
5.Manage Stock Items — CRUD ingredients: name, unit, low-stock threshold
6.Manage Menus — CRUD menus, each menu has ingredient template list (per pot/batch)
Google Sheets — 1 spreadsheet per month
📋 Purchases
date · store · item (FR) · qty · unit · price · total
📦 Stock
date · ingredient · amount · unit · reason · menu
💰 Daily Sales
date · menu · boxes · price/box · cash · card · total
📈 Monthly Summary
auto-calculated · total income · expenses · loss

Device & UX
