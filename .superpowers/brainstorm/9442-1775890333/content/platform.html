How will your client use this system?

This shapes everything — architecture, complexity, and cost

A

Web App (browser on phone/tablet)

She opens a URL in her browser. Works on any device. Good for daily data entry, seeing reports, uploading receipts.

B

LINE Bot (chat-based)

She chats with a bot in LINE. Very natural for Thai users. Send receipt photo → bot extracts and saves. Quick stock updates via messages.

C

Google Sheets only (no custom UI)

Everything lives in Google Sheets. A script processes uploaded receipts. Manual entry in the spreadsheet directly. Low maintenance, familiar interface.
